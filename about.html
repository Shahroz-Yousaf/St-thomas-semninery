About Us

Founders and Faculty

Bishop John Joseph

Founding Bishop

Bishop John Joseph established the seminary in 1981 to nurture vocations for the Diocese of Faisalabad. His vision for priestly formation continues to guide our institution.

Father Francis

First Rector (1981-1990)

As the seminary's first rector, Father Francis established the spiritual and academic foundations that continue to shape our formation program today.

Current Faculty

Our faculty consists of dedicated priests, religious, and lay scholars who bring both academic excellence and pastoral experience to their teaching.

  • Rev. Dr. Michael Thomas - Rector and Professor of Scripture
  • Rev. Peter James - Spiritual Director
  • Dr. Sarah Johnson - Professor of Church History
  • Rev. Matthew Wilson - Professor of Moral Theology

Accreditations and Achievements
