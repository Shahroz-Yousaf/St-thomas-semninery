Rector Welcome You

"Dear friends in Christ,

It is with great joy that I welcome you to St. Thomas the Apostle Minor Seminary, a place where young men discern God's call and prepare for priestly ministry. For over 40 years, our seminary has been forming men intellectually, spiritually, and pastorally to serve the Church in Pakistan and beyond.

May St. Thomas, our patron who came to believe through his doubt, inspire us all to deeper faith."

Yours in Christ,
Rev. Fr. Younis Inyant

Our Mission

St. Thomas the Apostle Minor Seminary serves as the preparatory seminary of the Diocese of Faisalabad, dedicated to nurturing young men in their spiritual, intellectual, and pastoral formation for potential service in the Catholic priesthood.
